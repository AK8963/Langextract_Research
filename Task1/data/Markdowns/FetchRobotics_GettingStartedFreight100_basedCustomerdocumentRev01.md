## Confidential Information

**!**

### Warning

Before you begin, read the safety and planning instructions on the Help Center, accessible through FetchCore. Failure to observe the safety warnings can result in personal injury or death.

## **1** Inspect the shipment for damages

Check the sensors mounted on the exterior of the box for mishandling. If you notice damage, contact Customer Support at fetchcare@fetchrobotics.com.

![](_page_0_Picture_9.jpeg)

**2**

### Unpack the robot

**A.** Unclasp and open the front panel. **B.** Create the ramp.

![](_page_0_Picture_13.jpeg)

For TagSurveyor robots: Align the front panel that you just removed with the velcro strips at the bottom of the crate to create the ramp.

For all other robots: Release the black safety strap, put the ramp support in place, and unfold the ramp until it is properly seated on the ground.

![](_page_0_Picture_17.jpeg)

**C.** Remove the wooden support brace. Tagsurveyor robots have an upper and lower brace.

![](_page_1_Picture_1.jpeg)

**D.** Use scissors to free the robot from the protective bag.

![](_page_1_Picture_3.jpeg)

**E.** Slowly roll the robot out of the crate and down the ramp.

![](_page_1_Picture_5.jpeg)

- **F.** Carefully remove any other packaging material from the robot. RollerTop robots: remove the risers between the RollerTop accessory and the Freight100 base.
- **G.** If included, remove all accessories kits from the crate.

![](_page_1_Picture_8.jpeg)

- **3** Turn on the robot
  - **A.** Set the battery disconnect switch to ON (I).

![](_page_1_Picture_11.jpeg)

**B.** Press the power button until the lights turn on.

![](_page_1_Picture_13.jpeg)

## **4** Connect the robot to WiFi

### Go to:

*company.fetchcore-cloud.com/management/#/wifi-setup*

Where *company* is replaced with the name of your server that was provided to you. Follow the on-screen instructions to connect your robot to Wi-Fi.
