# **LOG-IN TO SCANGUN**

To access the Oracle Mobile Warehouse Application (MWA) you need to log in to the scangun first.

#### **Step 1:** Power up the Scangun

Click on the 'Express Client' icon

The Express Client will be opened and will show the Intellinum connection information. Click on the button 'Connect'

![](_page_0_Picture_8.jpeg)

**Step 2:** The Oracle Login screen will be displayed to enter your 'User Name' and 'Password'

## **Important!**

Always use 'Enter' when you type any information into a field on the scangun

![](_page_0_Picture_12.jpeg)

### **Step 3:** The 'Main' Warehouse Management menu will now be shown

 From the Main Menu you can access the different sub-menus: For each Department you will see a separate sub-menu (identified by numbers 1-5 and 8)

The remaining sub-menus cover the following:

- 6. Inquiry: All Inquiry options
- 7. Label Printing: The menu-options required to print labels
- 9. Other: This sub-menu contains the menu-options that are not often used

![](_page_0_Picture_19.jpeg)

You are now ready to start working with the Mobile Application.
