## **LPN STATUS & ASSOCIATED TRANSACTIONS**

Throughout the lifecycle of an LPN, the LPN can go through different statuses. Below is a summary of the different statuses and the LPN transactions that can be done at each stage.

| LPN Status<br>(Context<br>Value) | Description                                                 | Comments                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | Transactions Allowed                                                                                           |
|----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1                                | Resides in<br>Inventory                                     | An LPN with a context of 1 indicates that material associated with this<br>LPN has been costed and accounted for in inventory                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | Standard or Inspection Routed<br>Receipt is not allowed<br>Direct Delivery Routed Receipt is                   |
|                                  |                                                             |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | allowed<br>All Inventory and Outbound<br>Picking and Packing Transactions<br>are allowed                       |
| 2                                | Resides in WIP                                              | An LPN with a context of 2 indicates that material associated with this<br>LPN is currently being transacted in WIP (Work in Process). Therefore,<br>the associated material is not yet in inventory and has not been costed<br>to inventory.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | WIP Drops into inventory can be<br>performed on LPNs in this context.                                          |
| 3                                | Resides in<br>Receiving                                     | An LPN with a context of 3 indicates that material associated with this<br>LPN have been received using a standard routing or inspection routing<br>receipt and have not been put away yet. Therefore, the associated<br>material is not yet in inventory and has not been costed to inventory.                                                                                                                                                                               | Used for Putaway from Receiving<br>into Inventory.                                                             |
| 4                                | Issued out of<br>Stores                                     | LPNs with a context of 4 are no longer tracked by the system, and<br>hence, no longer associated with a locator within the warehouse. The<br>system does however retain history information pertaining to<br>transactions this LPN was involved in and material it was associated with<br>prior to leaving the warehouse. LPNs shipped out of inventory receive<br>this context and may not be re-received.                                                                   | Only stored for historical<br>transactions. This cannot be<br>reused in the system for any<br>transaction.     |
| 5                                | Pre-generated                                               | When LPNs are pre-generated by Oracle WMS and have not been used<br>yet, they are not associated with any physical material. They can be<br>printed and used to identify material during any stage of the material<br>management process such as inbound, replenishment, outbound, and<br>so on. Essentially, this context refers to LPNs that are ready to be used.                                                                                                          | Can be used at any stage of the<br>material management process for<br>all operations                           |
| 6                                | Resides in<br>Intransit                                     | An LPN with this context is an indication that it is currently moving from<br>one location to another. Possible uses for this are when an LPN is moved<br>from one organization to the next, for example while the LPN is on a<br>truck. The LPN is in an intermediary state but resides within the entire<br>system. This context is used only for inter-org transit or internal sales<br>orders where an indirect shipping network is defined between the<br>organizations. | Cannot be used for active<br>transactions unless received in<br>destination and/or returned to<br>source       |
| 7                                | Resides in Vendor<br>Site                                   | When a vendor sends an Advanced Shipment Notice (ASN) to Oracle<br>WMS, the system internally generates LPNs and associates them with<br>material information on the ASN. These LPNs receive a context of 7.<br>Material associated with LPNs of this context are not on-hand or costed<br>until it is received.                                                                                                                                                              | Cannot be used for internal<br>warehouse transactions.<br>I do not believe Zebra uses LPNs in<br>this context. |
| 8                                | Packing context,<br>used as a<br>temporary context<br>value | This context is temporary and used internally by the software as an<br>intermediary. It should not be used or referenced anywhere including<br>the setup of picking or put away rules                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | Cannot be used for any warehouse<br>transactions.                                                              |
| 9                                | Loaded for<br>shipment                                      | An LPN loaded for shipment has just been loaded onto a carrier ready to<br>leave the warehouse. Once the entire carrier leaves the dock, the LPN<br>obtains a context of 6 Resides in Intransit or 4 Issued out of Stores.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | These LPNs can be only shipped<br>out of the dock or unloaded. No<br>other transactions can be done.           |
| 10                               | Prepack of WIP                                              | LPNs that reside in WIP and are associated with material that is pre<br>packed by WIP receive this context. This context adds an extra level of<br>granularity to an LPN's usage within WIP. This is used when the system<br>has associated the LPN with material and printed the labels, but the<br>material has not yet been physically packed.                                                                                                                             | I do not believe Zebra uses LPNs in<br>this context.                                                           |
| 11                               | Picked                                                      | LPNs picked during the picking process receive this context. They are<br>intransit within the warehouse.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | These LPNs can be dropped in the<br>destination locator, but no other<br>transactions can be done.             |
