## 1. OVERVIEW

This document outlines the procedure for resetting the F100 Robot.

### 2. CHARGING ERROR

✓ If a charging error occurs, the lightning bolt will be blinking red to reset follow the steps below.

![](_page_0_Picture_6.jpeg)

- **1.** Disconnect the charging cable.
  - **2.** Turn off the Robot, press and hold the power button for 5 seconds.
  - **3.** Turn off the breaker switch.
  - **4.** Wait for a minute or two.
  - **5.** Turn the breaker switch on again.
  - **6.** Press the power button again.
  - **7.** Connect that charging cable.
  - **8.** If the issue persists, look in the next connector to ensure there are no foreign objects obstructing the connection.

![](_page_0_Picture_15.jpeg)

**9.** Ensure that the Fetch logo is facing up.

![](_page_0_Picture_17.jpeg)
