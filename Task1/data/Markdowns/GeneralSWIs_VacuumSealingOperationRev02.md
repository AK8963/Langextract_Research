## 1. **OVERVIEW**

The following work instructions explains the workflow for vacuum sealing the moisture sensitive ESD components (BGAs, and ICs)

# 2. **VACUUM SEALING OPERATION**

- 1.When picking the partial (less than a full sealed tray/tube/reel) moisture sensitive ESD components like BGAs or ICs, the user will need to utilize a vacuum sealer to properly seal the components in a moisture protective ESD packaging.
- 2. Pick the sealed tray/tube/reel and bring it to the ESD station, refer to ESD Sensitive Parts Handling.
- 3. Open the sealed tray/tube/reel 1/16" below the seal level

![](_page_0_Picture_7.jpeg)

✓ Make sure to cut slightly below the seam to leave as much packaging for re-sealing after picking the partials.

![](_page_0_Picture_9.jpeg)

- 4. Take out the required quantity of components out of the sealed packaging using the vacuum pen. Press on the button to create a vacuum and pick up the part.
- 5. Place the components in a special tray, 1 part per a tray slot.

![](_page_0_Picture_12.jpeg)

6. Place the tray in a moisture protective ESD bag together with the desiccant and a moisture-indicating card.

![](_page_1_Picture_2.jpeg)

![](_page_1_Picture_3.jpeg)

7. Place the bag on the vacuum sealing machine, make sure to thread the vacuum valve between the bag edges as displayed.

![](_page_1_Picture_5.jpeg)

![](_page_1_Picture_6.jpeg)

8. Turn on the compressor by pulling the red button out.

![](_page_1_Picture_8.jpeg)

9. Turn on the vacuum sealer by pressing the switch.

![](_page_2_Picture_2.jpeg)

10. Press on the pedal and wait for the vacuum machine to suck the air out of the packaging.

![](_page_2_Picture_4.jpeg)

- 11. Release the pedal once the air is sucked out of the packaging, the light on the vacuum sealer will light up, indicating the heating process.
- 12. Once the vacuum sealer bar moves up and releases the packaging, it will be sealed.

![](_page_2_Picture_7.jpeg)

- 14. Shut off the compressor by pushing the red button in.

- 15. Power off the vacuum sealer by pressing the switch.
  - ✓ Compressor and vacuum sealer should be shut down after each use!
