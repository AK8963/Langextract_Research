## **OPERATING UNITS IN TX4-TX5**

Within Zebra Technologies, we use different Operating Units to cover the different (legal) entities. In the US there are 2 Operating Units:

- 1 Operating Unit for 'Purchasing' which is identified as 'ZTC' (Zebra Technologies Corporation) This Operating Unit also includes the Internal Sales Orders
- 1 Operating Unit for 'Sales' which is identified as 'ZTI' (Zebra Technologies International)

Within these Operating Units we use Inventory Organizations, which represent the different sites/warehouses. In the Kenosha DC we have the following Inventory Organizations:

- TX4: Kenosha DC Finished Goods/Hardware
- TX5: Kenosha DC Service Parts

Whenever you want to view Sales Order details (both Internal and External Sales Orders) you need to enable selection of the 'Operating Unit' to ensure you can see both types of Sales Orders.

**Step 1:** Navigate to 'WMS Inquiries' -> 'View Sales Order' -> 'Orders'

**Step 2:** Open the folder 'LT-Oper Unit 01' by clicking on the folder icon in the top left hand corner:

> *Note: Full details of how to work with the Oracle 'Folder' options is covered in the 'Oracle ERP - Basic Navigation Training'*

Click on 'OK'

**Step 3:** To ensure the menu-option is opened using this folder as the default folder, go to 'Folder' in the toolbar at the top

of your screen and select 'Save As'

![](_page_0_Picture_17.jpeg)

Tick the box for 'Open As Default' And click on 'OK'
