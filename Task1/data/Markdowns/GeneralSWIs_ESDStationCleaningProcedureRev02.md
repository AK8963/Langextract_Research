## 1. **OVERVIEW**

This work instruction describes the procedure of cleaning the ESD workstations.

## 2. **ESD STATIONS CLEANING PROCEDURE**

- 1. The ESD station needs to be cleaned daily at the end of every shift. The procedure is included in the daily 5S activity for the verification area.
- 2. Start the cleaning by removing the trash, extra supplies, and debris from the tables and the floor.
- 3. After removing the trash, empty the trash bins and return them to the designated spots, marked with white tape on the floor.

![](_page_0_Picture_7.jpeg)

- 4. Place all the equipment used at the stations to their designated locations (ESD tape, scanners, supplies, vacuum pen, scissors, ESD wrist ties, tape guns).
- 5. Refill the supplies at each station if they are low/empty.

![](_page_0_Picture_10.jpeg)

- 7. Spray and wipe the workstation area with the ESD spray.

8. Make sure that the vacuum sealer, compressor, and reel counter are powered off.
