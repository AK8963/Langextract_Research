WI – PTO - Overpack Bulk Printing for Wal-Mart and Toshiba

#### **OVERPACK BULK PRINTING FOR WALMART AND TOSHIBA**

**1.** Logged into Oracle ERP, open the folder 'ZTC Warehouse System Packer' and select the option 'Packing Workbench'

![](_page_0_Picture_5.jpeg)

**2.** Select the organization from where the pick was made; ex. 'TX4' Click OK

![](_page_0_Picture_7.jpeg)

**3.** Proceed to the 'View' tab and click on 'Requests'

![](_page_0_Picture_9.jpeg)

Document: WI – PTO – Overpack Bulk Printing for Wal-Mart and Toshiba Rev01 Page 1 of 5 September 27, 2024

**4.** Select 'Submit a New Request'

**5.** In the Name Bar enter '%bulk%' Click the 'Submit'

**6.** Select the option 'Zebra Shipping Bulk Custom Label Program'

Then click 'OK'

![](_page_1_Picture_5.jpeg)

**7.** 'Zebra Shipping Bulk Custom Label Program' will appear in the 'Name' field In the additional pop-up that reads 'Enter a value for Organization parameter', click 'OK'

![](_page_2_Picture_2.jpeg)

**8. Fill out the parameter form** with the required information from the PCR:

**Organization**: TX4

**Label Format Type**: Click on the three dots '**...**' and select one of the following:

\* For Toshiba: **TOSHIBA SHIPPING LABEL** 

**\*** For Walmart: **WALMART STARCOMPLEX BULK KIT LABEL**

\* For Wal-Mart: **WALMART STARCOMPLEX BULK OVERPACK**

**Quantity**: Enter the quantity required on the label, *not the Order Quantity on the PCR.*

*The quantity would be found on the kit/overpack label, not the Order Quantity on the PCR*

**Delivery Number**: Type in do not scan. Delivery Num is on the PCR sheet.

**Part Number**: Click the three dots '⋮' and select the item you need the label for

*If there are multiple Part Numbers to choose from select the corresponding name from the 'Item' list in the PCR along with the 'Picked Quantity'*

**Printer Name**: Type in the printer number (ex: z58p231)

**Copies**: Enter the number of labels needed

*If multiple labels are needed, divide the Order Quantity by the quantity indicated on the Overpack*

*\*Customer is indicated on PCR*

![](_page_3_Picture_2.jpeg)

*\*Label Format Type*

![](_page_3_Picture_4.jpeg)

#### *Label Reminder:*

Toshiba orders require **1" (L) x 3" (W)** labels Wal-Mart orders require **2" (L) x 4" (W)** labels

- **9.** Once you click 'Ok' on the Parameters page, the Pararmeters field should be filled out on the 'Submit New Request' field.
- **10.** Click 'Submit' and a label should print.
