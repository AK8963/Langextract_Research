# 1. OVERVIEW

The purpose of this work instruction is to provide a guide for cleaning the Fetch models utilizing cleaning materials that will not damage the Robot and its components.

# 2. CLEANING MATERIALS

![](_page_0_Picture_5.jpeg)

![](_page_0_Picture_6.jpeg)

- **1.** To clean fingerprints, dirt, and smudges from the skins of Fetch use a clean Microfiber cloth, soft, less lint paper towel, and/or IPA alcohol or Windex.
- **2.** Make sure to spray/wet the cloth with the isopropyl alcohol or Windex, and then gently clean the skins of the robot.

#### **Windex**:

An all-around cleaner used to remove fingerprints.

### **IPA Alcohol**:

Mainly use on the "WHITE" skins and white surfaces (helps remove any dark spots and hard stains on the skins).

- ✓ Do not spray or pour Windex or IPA alcohol directly on the skins or sensor lenses of the Robot, this may damage the skins or worse cause fluids to enter the Robot.
- ✓ Do not use Windex and/or alcohol on sensor lenses or cameras.

### **Soft, Less Lint Paper Towel**:

An all-around towel. Use for straining excess liquid (if any), drying, wiping, dusting, and cleaning up without scratching up the product surface.

✓ Do not use abrasive cloths on the skins it may cause scratches.

#### **Microfiber Duster, Bench Brush Medium Bristle, or Small Vacuum:**

Can be used to clean the crate before shipping it to the Customer.
