## 1. OVERVIEW

This document outlines specifications and standards for all cosmetic surface applications of Fetch Robotics.

## 2. COSMETIC STANDARDS

#### **Cosmetic Class Requirements**

All cosmetic finishes will be subject to Class "A" unless stated otherwise in Fetch Robotics engineering drawings, formal specifications, and other reference documents.

#### **Surface Preparation:**

- All surfaces must be cleaned to render them free of dust, grease, oil, rust, or other contamination.
- All surface requiring Class A and B finish must be free of nicks, dents, scratches, metal dust and other tool marks before application of coating is applied
- All holes and edges must be deburred using conventional methods to remove sharp edges.

#### **Finish Specifications:**

The total thickness of coating and substrate will be within the range of 0.003'' to 0.005''.

#### **Inspection**

Class A and Class B cosmetic surface finish shall be subject to a 100% inspection rate.

#### **Time and Viewing Distance:**
