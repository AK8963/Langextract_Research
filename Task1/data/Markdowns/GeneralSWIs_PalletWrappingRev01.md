## 1. OVERVIEW

This work instruction provides information on how to wrap products on a pallet using the shrink-wrap machine.

#### 2. EQUIPMENT INSPECTION

- **1.** Before the Shrink Wrap Machine can be turned on, inspect the equipment.
  - Inspect the power cord and plug for any signs of damage.
  - Inspect the Control Panel to make sure all control knobs and buttons are attached.
  - Inspect the turn table for damage (bent, denting, etc.) that may impact a safe operation.
    - ✓ The "Emergency Stop" button MUST be pushed in, and the Shrink Wrap machine is turned off before equipment inspection.

#### 3. PALLET INSPECTION

- **1.** Complete a visual inspection of the pallet to ensure it is free of damage and structurally sound.
- **2.** Heat-Treated (HT) Pallets are used for any international shipments.
- **3.** Plastic pallets are used for Brazil shipments.
- **4.** Boxes on the pallet should be free of damage and not exceed 1-inch max overhang of the pallet.
  - ✓ Reference the *Acceptable and Unacceptable Pallet Job Aid.*
- **5.** Heavier boxes should be at the base of the pallet and lighter boxes on top.
  - ✓ Heavier boxes should never be on top of lighter boxes.
- **6.** Boxes containing Dangerous Goods should be properly labeled prior to wrapping.

#### 4. SHRINK-WRAP MACHINE

- **1.** Place the pallet on the turn table.
  - ✓ Manual pallet jack must be removed from the machine turn table.
- **2.** Grab the shrink wrap and tie it to the bottom of the pallet.
- **3.** Make sure all the settings on the control panel are set to the specific needs of the pallet you are wrapping and press the **GREEN** start button.
  - ✓ If you notice any discrepancies, push the "Emergency Stop" button, and alert management and/or maintenance.
  - ✓ If you need to replace the shrink wrap, push the "Emergency Stop" button, and replace the shrink wrap.
- **4.** Pallet wrapping is complete when the pallet is completely wrapped, and the turn table has stopped spinning.
- **5.** Remove the pallet off the Shrink Wrap machine turn table and placed it in the staging area.
