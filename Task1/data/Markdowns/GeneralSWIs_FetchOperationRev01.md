# 3. SAFETY

- **1.** Remember that the Robot is a Power Industrial Vehicle; therefore, you must follow the appropriate safety protocols when working around it.
  - A. Remove any obstruction in the work area.
  - B. When the Robot is in motion, give ample space even though the Robot has sensors to avoid collisions.
  - C. Periodically check for damage to the Robot.
- **2.** Understand the Robot's capabilities and limitations.
- **3.** Adapt to the Robot's pace.
- **4.** For Any issues or concerns, seek the Supervisor's assistance.

## 4. INBOUND

- **1.** Follow the normal receiving process.
- **2.** Determine if you would be utilizing the cart as Pack1.
- **3.** If using the cart, select the appropriate workflow on the Zebra tablet.
- **4.** Follow the normal putaway process.
- **5.** Once the putaway is complete and the cart is empty, manually move to the assigned location.

## 5. KITTING

- **1.** Follow the normal Kitting process.
- **2.** If the kitted order is shipping via parcel, place the product on the cart and send it to the parcel area.
- **3.** Select the appropriate workflow.

## 6. PICKING

- **1.** Follow the normal picking process.
- **2.** When picking, decide if you will pick to the cart, a case/box, or a pallet.
- **3.** If you pick to the cart, put the product on the cart and send it to the parcel area.
  - ✓ Ensure the product was dropped correctly to a Cone; otherwise, it will cause issues with manifesting.

## 7. PARCEL

- **1.** When receiving a product on a Fetch Cart, ensure the product belongs in the parcel area.
- **2.** Verify the product against the PCR.

# 8. TRASH

- **1.** If using a Robot to assist with trash, ensure the CartConnect has the appropriate crate.
- **2.** If the crate is full, select the appropriate workflow.
- **3.** The cart will proceed to the compactors.
- **4.** When done emptying the crate, ensure the crate is in its designated location.
