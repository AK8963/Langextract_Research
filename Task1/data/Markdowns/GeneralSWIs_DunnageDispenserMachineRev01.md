## 1. OVERVIEW

The purpose of this Work Instruction is to show how to use and load dunnage paper into RanPak Wrap Pak PT and Fill PAK TT machines. The Wrap Pak PT machine is available to use for all associates in the LTL Packing area. This machine dispenses dunnage, which consists of two paper pieces folded together and cut to the user's specified length and quantity. Fill Pak TT stations are located throughout the Distribution Center and must be reloaded regularly by the attending associate.

## 2. RANDPAK WRAPPK PT USAGE

- **1.** The homepage will display several actions that may be taken: individually dispense dunnage (pedal symbol), Batch (selected quantity will be cut), and EDS (customizable length).
- **2.** Selecting the 'Pedal Symbol' will allow associates to dispense singlecut pieces of dunnage; the length will be adjustable.
  - A) Step on the pedal below and collect cut dunnage
- **3.** The 'Batch' setting will allow a specific quantity and length to be cut; the 'Length' and 'Pad quantity' will be adjustable.
  - B) Once the correct specifications are determined, tap the 'Run' button, 'Pad Count' will count the number of pieces dispensed.

## 3. RANPAK WRAP PAK PT LOADING

- **1.** Two stacks of dunnage are required for the machine to operate properly, and it is the responsibility of the associate to replace the paper as needed.
- **2.** First, the top compartment must be opened.
  - A) Push the lever down
  - B) Manually bring the 'door' up
- **3.** Remove the guard.

![](_page_1_Picture_7.jpeg)

- **4.** Next, take two stacks of dunnage paper, placing them in their individual platforms.
- **5.** Begin unfolding the dunnage paper from both stacks, join them together.
- **6.** Insert the joined pieces of paper between carefully in between the two rolls first, then between the two smaller rolls above.

## 4. RANPAK FILL PAK TT LOADING

- **1.** Before any disassembly or loading, the power must be turned off.
- **2.** Remove the top hood.

![](_page_2_Picture_10.jpeg)

- **3.** Take the stack of dunnage paper and press the two sides of the end piece together, creating a cone shape
- **4.** Push the scrunch paper through the top of the machine until it comes clearly out towards the wheel mechanisms.
- **5.** Carefully push the pinched-together paper into the wheels
  - A) Manually turn the wheels until it 'catches' the dunnage
