## **LPN INQUIRY**

The LPN Inquiry menu-option can be used to check the status and contents for a particular LPN.

**Step 1:** Using the Mobile Application, navigate to 'Inquiry-> 'LPN'

**Step 2:** Enter the correct Organization and press 'Enter' on the keypad

**Step 3:** In the LPN field, scan the LPN and click on 'Find LPN'

![](_page_0_Picture_8.jpeg)

![](_page_0_Picture_9.jpeg)

**Step 4:** The scangun will display the Location and the state of the LPN, for example:

- Defined but not used: The LPN is defined but not yet in use
- Resides in Receiving: The materials are being received
- Resides in Inventory: The materials are in stock
- Picked: The materials have been picked for a Sales Order
- Packing context: The materials have been loaded but not yet dropped
- Resides in WIP: The materials are being assembled on a Work Order
- Issued Out of Stores: The materials are no longer in the Warehouse

![](_page_0_Figure_18.jpeg)

The 'LPN Details' button shows some characteristics of the LPN

![](_page_0_Figure_20.jpeg)

The 'LPN Contents' button can be used to find more information on the LPN

![](_page_0_Figure_22.jpeg)

Use the buttons 'Next' and 'Previous' to find information on other items within the LPN. These buttons are only visible if the LPN contains multiple items.

The 'SN' button can be used to view the serial numbers:
