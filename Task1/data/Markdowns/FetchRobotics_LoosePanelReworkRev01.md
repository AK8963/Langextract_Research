### 1. OVERVIEW

This document outlines the procedure for reworking the side panel of the F100 Robot base.

### 2. PANEL REWORK

**1.** Identify the panel that needs rework.

![](_page_0_Picture_6.jpeg)

![](_page_0_Picture_7.jpeg)

![](_page_0_Picture_8.jpeg)

**2.** Loosen the screw to the extent where we can easily move the front and side panels for the rework.

![](_page_0_Picture_10.jpeg)

- **3.** Overlap the side panel to the front panel by pushing the panels together.
  - ✓ The bottom tab of the side panel should be properly aligned with the front panel.

![](_page_0_Picture_13.jpeg)

*This document contains proprietary information which is the exclusive property of Zebra Technologies Corp.*

**4.** Hold the panels in place by hand or with tape (Tesa appliance tape).

#### **The are 2 ways to tighten the screw:**

a. Torque screw manually by hand until tight

![](_page_1_Picture_3.jpeg)

![](_page_1_Picture_4.jpeg)

- b. Use an electric driver on level 2 and a high setting.
  - ✓ Be mindful of the force being used, too much could potentially cause a threated screw.

![](_page_1_Picture_7.jpeg)

![](_page_1_Picture_8.jpeg)

- **5.** Ensure the rework is complete by gently pulling the bottom of the panel.
  - ✓ It should not be loose.

![](_page_1_Picture_11.jpeg)

*This document contains proprietary information which is the exclusive property of Zebra Technologies Corp.*

### **6.** Rework completed.
