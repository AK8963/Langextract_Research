## Confidential Information

## Creating an Offline Shipment in GTM

Version 1.1

- 1) Navigate yourself to the GTM website[: https://otmgtm-a577711.otm.us2.oraclecloud.com](https://otmgtm-a577711.otm.us2.oraclecloud.com/) -If asked for a password please use your Zebra's login
- 2) Click on **Offline Shipment** located on the right side of the window

![](_page_1_Picture_2.jpeg)

3) On the next window, click on **New**

![](_page_1_Picture_4.jpeg)

4) On the next window, fill out the following fields:

![](_page_2_Figure_1.jpeg)

1. **Method of Shipment**

Carrier name

- 2. **Transport Mode** Select from list
- 3. **INCO Term** Select from list
- 4. **INCO Term Location** City Name based on INCO term
- 5. **Reference Number** Tracking number
- 6. **Ship To Contact** Name and Phone number
- 7. **Ship From Contact** Name and Phone number
- 8. **Shipment Date**

- Select from the pop-up calendar
- 9. **Total Cartons** Value must be an integer
- 10. **Total Gross Weight** Value must be a number
- 11. **Gross Weight UOM** Select from dropdown

## 12. **Involved Parties (Ship From, Bill To, and Ship To)**

- i) Select the appropriate party from the **Involved Party Qualifier ID** dropdown options
- ii) Click on the **F** to search for **Involved Party**
- iii) Fill in as many field as possible and actively use the criteria of "Begins with", "Contains", etc.
- iv) Then hit **Search** and select the correct option. Then **Finish**, then **Save**
- v) Do all steps for all three parties
- 13. **Ports** (NALA region only)
  - i) Select **Port of Export** from the **Port Qualifier ID** dropdown options
  - ii) Click **F** to search for **Port ID** then **Save**
- 14. Click on **Finish** to save your work.

5) Save the Offline Shipment number and click **Edit**.

![](_page_3_Picture_1.jpeg)

6) Click **Transaction Line** at the bottom of the page then click **New Transaction Line**.

![](_page_3_Figure_3.jpeg)

7) Click **the magnifier** of the **Item ID** to search for the part.

![](_page_3_Figure_5.jpeg)

- 8) Input the Zebra part number in the **Item Name** field then search.
- 9) Select the right item from the next window and hit **Finish**. (Notice that **Item ID** is not populated)
- 10) Proceed to filling out the fields

![](_page_4_Figure_3.jpeg)

- **Country of Origin** Three letter Country code, click **l** to see list
- **Declared Value** Unit price of item

- **Shipped Quantity** Number of items that is being shipped
- **UOM** Usually **EA** for each or select from dropdown

- 11) Then click **Save**.
- 12) Repeat **Step 7 thru 12** for all the items you are shipping.
- 13) Then hit **Finish**.
- 14) Copy/Note down the shipment number and check the great box.
- 15) Select **Actions** and **Submit Offline Shipment**.

![](_page_4_Figure_13.jpeg)

16) Go back to the home screen and find your transaction by entering the **transaction ID** and press **Search**.

![](_page_5_Figure_1.jpeg)

- 18) If the **Indicator** is **Red**, please send the shipment number to GTCO for them to release the hold.
- 19) If the **Indicator** is **Green**, or when you get confirmation that hold has been released by GTCO, proceed to the next steps.

![](_page_5_Figure_4.jpeg)

20) Search your shipment in **Offline Shipment** and go into it.

![](_page_6_Figure_1.jpeg)

- 21) Select your shipment (most likely the only one that shows up).
- 22) Then
  - 1. Go into the **Documents** tab.
  - 2. Click on the **3 squares** logo on the left side of the Invoice and Packing List rows.
  - Click on the Generate Document from the pop-up menu for each document.

![](_page_6_Figure_7.jpeg)

23) Don't forget to Sign and Date the invoice before proceeding with the shipment
